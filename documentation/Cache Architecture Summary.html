üéØ Key Takeaways

Different Data, Different Places
Raw data in KV, processed pages in Worker cache, final responses at Edge
Layered Performance
Multiple fallback layers ensure fast responses even when some caches miss
Geographic Distribution
Your content is cached near users worldwide for consistent performance
Automatic Management
Caches fill and expire automatically based on your TTL settings
